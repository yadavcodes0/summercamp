Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Welcome to the Kids Workshop 2026 mobile application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our App to register a child for the workshop, we collect the following personal information:

  • Parent/Guardian Name: To identify the primary contact.
  • Phone Number: To contact you in case of emergencies and for verification purposes.
  • Child's Name & Age: To assign them to the appropriate workshop activities.
  • Address: For workshop records and logistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used for the administration and safety of the Kids Workshop 2026. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Register your child for the workshop.
  • Generate a unique QR code for secure daily check-in and check-out.
  • Contact you during the workshop regarding your child's well-being or schedule updates.
  • Maintain accurate attendance records for workshop security.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in our backend database (Supabase). We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal information. Only authorized camp administrators have access to this data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively by the Kids Workshop administration team for the duration of the workshop.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, which is typically until the conclusion of the workshop and any immediate follow-up activities. After that, we securely delete or anonymize your data unless required by law to retain it.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is designed to collect information about children only from their parents or legal guardians. We never collect data directly from children. By using the App, you verify that you have the legal authority to provide this information about your child.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact the workshop administration desk at the Sant Nirankari Satsang Bhawan, Inderlok.
